# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is NovaSaaS?

NovaSaaS is a B2B project management platform designed for teams of all sizes. It helps you organize tasks, collaborate with team members, and track project progress.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of our Pro plan. No credit card required.

### What platforms do you support?

NovaSaaS works on:
- Web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- iOS app (iPhone and iPad)
- Android app
- Desktop apps for Windows and macOS

### Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export all your data in CSV or JSON format from Settings > Data > Export.

## Pricing & Billing

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (Enterprise plans only)

### Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from Settings > Billing.

### Do you offer discounts?

We offer:
- 20% discount for annual billing
- 50% discount for nonprofits
- Special pricing for educational institutions

### What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

You'll receive a notification when approaching limits. If exceeded:
- Free plan: Upgrade required to continue
- Paid plans: Overage charges apply or auto-upgrade

## Features

### How many team members can I add?

| Plan | Team Members |
|------|--------------|
| Free | Up to 5 |
| Pro | Up to 50 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

### Do you offer Gantt charts?

Yes, Gantt charts are available in Pro and Enterprise plans.

### Can I create custom fields?

Custom fields are available in Pro and Enterprise plans.

### Do you support time tracking?

Yes, built-in time tracking is available in all paid plans.

### Can I set up recurring tasks?

Yes, recurring tasks are supported in Pro and Enterprise plans.

## Security & Privacy

### Where is my data stored?

All data is stored in secure, SOC 2 certified data centers in the US and EU.

### Is NovaSaaS GDPR compliant?

Yes, we are fully GDPR compliant. You can request data export or deletion at any time.

### Do you offer two-factor authentication?

Yes, 2FA is available for all accounts using authenticator apps or SMS.

### How often do you backup data?

We perform continuous backups with point-in-time recovery capabilities.
